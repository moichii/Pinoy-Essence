"Dishes"

Adobo

1. Chicken or Pork (common choices, but other meats like beef or seafood can also be used)
2. Soy Sauce
3. Vinegar (usually white vinegar or cane vinegar)
4. Garlic (minced or crushed)
5. Bay Leaves (dried or fresh)
6. Whole Peppercorns (black or white)
7. Salt (to taste)
8. Water or Chicken/Pork Broth (optional, for added liquid)

Bulalo

- 2 lbs beef shank with bone marrow
- 1 onion, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
- 2 stalks lemongrass, pounded
- 1 corn on the cob, cut into chunks
- 1/2 head of cabbage, sliced
- 2 potatoes, peeled and quartered
- 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
- 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
- Fish sauce or salt, to taste
- Water, enough to cover the meat and vegetables
- Scallions, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Sisig

1. Pork face, jowls, belly and shoulder: 500 grams
2. Onion: 1 medium-sized
3. Garlic: 3 cloves
4. Calamansi Juice: 1/4 cup
5. Soy Sauce: 2 tablespoons
6. Vinegar: 2 tablespoons
7. Red or Green Chili Peppers: 2 pieces
8. Salt: 1 teaspoon
9. Black Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
10. Mayonnaise: 2 tablespoons
11. Butter: 1 tablespoon
12. Pig's Brain or Liver
13. Egg

Kare Kare

1. 2 pounds (900g) oxtail, cut into sections
2. 1 pound (450g) beef tripe,
3. 1/2 cup peanut butter
4. 2 tablespoons annatto seeds (achuete)
5. 2 tablespoons cooking oil
6. 1 onion, chopped
7. 4 cloves garlic, minced
8. 4 cups water
9. 1 bunch bok choy or pechay (Chinese cabbage)
10. 1 medium-sized eggplant, sliced
11. 1 bundle long beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
12. 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
13. 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
14. Salt and pepper to taste
Optional garnishes: - Crushed peanuts - Fried garlic

Lechon Kawali

Pork belly: Approximately 2-3 pounds (1-1.5 kg) of pork belly,
Water: Sufficient amount to boil the pork belly.
Salt
Pepper
Garlic: crushed or minced.
Bay leaves: 2-3 pieces of bay leaves.
Cooking oil
Soy sauce: Around 1/4 cup for dipping sauce.
Vinegar: Approximately 1/4 cup
Onion: 1 small onion, thinly sliced, for garnishing.
Chili peppers: 2-3 chili peppers, sliced or chopped, for spice.
Mang Tomas sauce: As needed, typically 1/2 cup or more, for serving with Lechon Kawali.

Caldereta

Developer1. Meat: Goat, beef, or chicken (cut into chunks)
2. Cooking oil
3. Garlic (minced)
4. Onion (chopped)
5. Tomato sauce or tomato paste
6. Potatoes (cubed)
7. Carrots (sliced)
8. Bell peppers (sliced)
9. Green olives (optional)
10. Liver spread (optional)
11. Red chili peppers (optional, for heat)
12. Beef or chicken broth (or water)
13. Fish sauce (or salt) and ground black pepper (to taste)
14. Grated cheese (such as cheddar or quickmelt) for topping (optional)

Tinola

Chicken: Approximately whole chicken (around 3-4 pounds) or 4-6 pieces of chicken
Green Papaya or Chayote: 1 medium-sized green papaya or 2-3 chayote squash
Ginger: About 1 thumb-sized piece of fresh ginger root (around 1 inch)
Garlic: 4-6 cloves of garlic
. Onion: 1 medium-sized onion
Fish Sauce: 2-3 tablespoons of fish sauce (patis),
Chicken Broth or Water: 4-6 cups of chicken broth or water
Spinach or Malunggay Leaves
Long Green Chili Peppers: 2-3 long green chili peppers
Cooking Oil: 2 tablespoons of cooking oil, such as vegetable oil.
Salt and Pepper

Pinakbet

1. 1 cup bitter melon (ampalaya),
2. 1 cup eggplant, sliced
3. 1 cup squash (kalabasa), cubed
4. 1 cup okra, whole or sliced
5. 1 cup long beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
6. 1 cup tomatoes, sliced
7. 1 onion, chopped
8. 4 cloves garlic, minced
9. 2 tablespoons cooking oil
10. 1 cup pork belly or shrimp (optional), cut into small pieces
11. 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
12. 1 cup water or vegetable broth
13. Salt and pepper to taste

Bicol Express

1. Pork: 500 grams, thinly sliced (traditionally, pork belly or shoulder is used)
2. Coconut Milk: 2 cups (fresh or canned)
3. Shrimp Paste (Bagoong): 2 tablespoons
4. Coconut Oil or Cooking Oil: 2 tablespoons
5. Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
6. Onion: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
7. Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated or minced
8. Red Chili Peppers: 4 to 6 pieces (adjust according to your spice preference), sliced diagonally
9. Long Green Chili Peppers: 4 to 6 pieces, sliced diagonally
10. Fish Sauce (Patis): 1 tablespoon (optional, for additional seasoning)
11. Salt: To taste
12. Ground Black Pepper: To taste

Sinigang na Hipon

1. Shrimp: 1 pound (peeled and deveined)
2. Tamarind Soup Base: 1 packet or fresh tamarind pulp (around 1 cup)
3. Water: 6 to 8 cups
4. Onion: 1 medium-sized (sliced)
5. Tomatoes: 2 medium-sized (quartered)
6. Green beans: 1 cup
7. Radish: 1 medium-sized
8. Eggplant: 1 medium-sized (sliced into thick rounds)
9. Kangkong (Water Spinach): 1 bunch
10. Okra: 1 cup (trimmed and halved)
11. Long green chili peppers: 2 to 3 pieces
12. Fish sauce (Patis): 3 to 4 tablespoons
13. Salt: To taste
14. Ground black pepper: To taste
15. Cooking oil: 2
16. Garlic: 3 cloves (minced)
